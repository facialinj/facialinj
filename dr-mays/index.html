I’m a D cup, what’s a good implant size to go for?
D cup
May 10, 2018
Thanks for your question. Choosing an implant size is a decision made by you and your surgeon at your consultation. The size selected is based on many factors such as your breast measurement, chest wall projection, skin elasticity, and nipple position.
