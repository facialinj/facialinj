Can "duck lip" from filler be fixed with more filler?
Filler correction
Jun 19, 2020
Thank you for your question. I would recommend going to a board-certified dermatologist for an evaluation. Depending on the size and shape of the lip, and how the initial filler was placed, sometimes more filler can fix the appearance. Other times it may need to be dissolved with hyaluronidase. We have our patients wait two weeks before they can they can have the area injected again. With proper technique your result should not have a “duck lip”.

When can one have Emsculpt after giving birth?
Emsculpt after pregnancy
Apr 16, 2020
Thank you for your question and congratulations on your new baby! Along with improving muscle tone and burning fat, Emsculpt can also help treat “diastasis recti” also known as the separation of the abdominal muscles. This is common during pregnancy. It’s best to wait at least 3-6 months post partum and no longer breastfeeding to begin any treatment. Please seek a board-certified dermatologist to determine if you are an ideal candidate for this treatment at that time.

Can you get PRP treatments while pregnant for androgenetic alopecia?
PRP while pregnant
Apr 16, 2020
Thank
you for your question. Many women during pregnancy, or post-partum experience
hair thinning or hair loss. No clinical studies have been performed
using PRP in pregnant or breast feeding women, therefore,
