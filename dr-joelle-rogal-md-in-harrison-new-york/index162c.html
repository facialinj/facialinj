Will fat injections help my sagging skin?
Fat grafting
Jul 5, 2019
Unfortunately, fat grafting to the face will only help restore volume to the face– it will not help with sagging skin.  Blepharoplasty (eye lift) and facelifts are two surgeries that can help with sagging skin.  Sometimes fat grafting is used in combination with these surgeries to achieve the most desirable result.
