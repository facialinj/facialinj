How long should I wait to have Botox and fillers after an infection in the soft tissue of my face?
Injectables following dental infections
Aug 27, 2020
It is best to wait a minimum of 2 weeks following complete resolution of your dental infection prior to proceeding with any facial injections. This is particularly true with filler as bacteria from the dental infection can spread to the filler and can cause a significant infection that not only will compromise your result, but can jeopardize your health as well!

Aug 26, 2020
Thank you for the question. In general, using a cannula to place filler under the eye (typically into the tear trough) is not significantly traumatic to the muscles of the cheek.
