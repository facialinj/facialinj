Blue/green vein near eye. What is the best treatment?
Blue vein near eye
Sep 13, 2020
These periorbital veins are quite common. They tend to be a bit deeper which leads to the bluish coloration. I use the Cutera Excel 1064nm laser to treat these vessels. Patients respond well to this treatment. The vein is eventually resorbed by the body and is not needed for circulation in this area. Please consult a board-certified dermatologist experienced in vascular laser treatments. Best wishes.

Sep 13, 2020
Botox can be used to lower the arch of the brow. It only takes a few units into the forehead muscle to achieve this result. The procedure is easy to do in the office and takes about 2 weeks to see the full benefit. Please consult a board-certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon experienced in aesthetic injectables. Best wishes.
