Lip Filler

What do dermal fillers feel like when light pressure is applied?
Filler
Sep 29, 2020
Thank you for your question. After getting dermal fillers especially in the chin we ask that you do not apply pressure or rest on the area for at least 2 days until the filler settles. So if possible, you would need to sleep on your back after having fillers to minimize the risk of the filler being moved. The product will not feel super firm or noticeable to the touch unless you develop lumps/bumps. Hope this helps!
