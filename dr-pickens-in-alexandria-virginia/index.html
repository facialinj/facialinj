Chubby cheeks, lines around mouth. Will fillers hold it up or make it look bigger?
Chubby cheeks and filler
Sep 22, 2020
It may sound counterintuitive but filler can actually bring definition to the face and cause a slenderizing effect and lift. Carefully discuss your concerns during a consultation with and injector who will map out for you what to expect. I would recommend a hyaluronic acid filler for your first time since it can be removed.
Are the masseters causing my jaw asymmetry and would Botox reduce it?
Jaw asymmetry
Sep 22, 2020
Thanks for your question and photo. The masseter muscle bulkiness is something that can be determined by a medical history and exam. It is usually associated with grinding of teeth, jaw pain and clicking. Other muscles are felt during the exam and the temporomandibular joint (TMJ). If this is the cause, Botox or similar injections (Dysport, Xeomin, Jueveau) can help with contouring and symptoms.
Would it be okay inject Botox/fat to my chin after Gore-Tex implant?
Injections to chin after Gore-Tex implant
Sep 22, 2020
Although a foreign body will increase the risk and severity of an infection the risk can certainly be reduced by using aseptic technique. Avoid shaving in the area the week before to avoid microscopic breaks in the skin which could be an entry point for bacteria.
Should I be scared of Botox? Can it kill me? Or stop my breathing? Can it cause blindness?
Scared of Botox
Sep 15, 2020
The reason why Botox is the # 1 nonsurgical cosmetic procedure is because it has such a high patient satisfaction and safety profile. These reasons are why it is used in virtually every specialty in medicine. It is one of the most studied medications we have.

It was originally used in children before cosmetic use because it is so safe and effective in large doses for cerebral palsy, polio, neck spasms and crossed eyes. The deadly side effects from distant spread have not been reported...

Can I have laser hair removal the day before surgery on the same spot?
Laser hair removal day prior to surgery
Sep 15, 2020
I would postpone laser hair removal prior to the surgery because shaving prior to surgery has been shown to increase the risk of wound infection. Also laser hair removal alters your skin barrier and if any untoward blister or burn should happen it could adversely affect your surgery causing it to be canceled.
Should I dissolve my botched jawline filler or wait it out?
Voluma in the jawline
Sep 15, 2020
It is still very early in the healing process after your injection with a more volumizing filler like Voluma. Your doctor can still massage it while it is malleable especially if warmed with a compress for a few minutes. It would be good to see your before photos. Give is some time to see how it looks in a few weeks if you can wait and then compare before photos your doctor took side by side to some after photos then discuss with your doctor.
What is the best treatment and filler for post surgical scar around my lips?
Postop Lip Scarring
Sep 7, 2020
You do not mention how long after surgery this photo was taken. Microneedling and PRP can help with wound healing. Hyaluronic acid filler of which there are many that are suitable can be used for lip enhancement or shaping but not necessary for wound healing. There are also topical applications of peptides and growth factors that can be used.
If PRP treatment is done under the eyes differently to each eye will the results be different under
PRP under the eyes
Sep 7, 2020
It is unclear what is meant by "differently" under each eye. Unsure if amount of fluid, method, etc. We are not exactly the same on both sides of any part of our body so the injections may require some slight variation, however PRP is used to stimulate your own body's cell growth. It may be used in combination with filler.
Do you recommend treatment straight into the folds and or into the cheeks?
Treatment of folds
Sep 7, 2020
It is best to treat the face for rejuvenation, not a fold. The fold is created because of volume loss and sagging as we start to lose collagen and fat pad displacement. For the sake of facial balance and a natural appearance it is best to rejuvenate the face not concentrate on the resulting area of the fold in isolation. Once the midface is lifted, the fold will require less correction. More can be determined from a thorough examination and more photos at different angles and at rest.
Does a cannula weaken or comprise cheek muscles when having under eye fillers via the cheek?
Cannulas for under eye fillers
Sep 7, 2020
No, cannulas will not weaken cheek muscles when entering the cheek to tunnel to the under eye area or otherwise. It is a safe technique and the manipulation will stimulate collagen formation to support the underlying structures. Good question that others may be concerned about also.
Can Dysport make a difference in sliming the lower shape of my face?
Can Dysport slim my lower face
Aug 24, 2020
Dysport injected into the masseter or chewing muscle that can become bulky usually due to grinding of the teeth will slim the face and over time recontour the face. An examination will reveal if you are a good candidate. It can also help jaw pain and headaches if present.
How many units of Dysport are required on each side to lift the corners of the mouth?
Lift corners of the mouth with Dysport
Aug 24, 2020
To treat a downturned mouth I usually use a 2 step process because it is caused by muscle activity and loss of lower face volume. For Dysport, I will use a range of 12-18 units to each side and also filler to restore volume. This can be done in the same sitting.
Can PRP Vampire (for face) make facial hair grow more?
PRP for face growing facial hair
Jul 21, 2020
I have done many vampire facelifts and facials and have yet to have anyone complain of facial hair growth. It may be a possible risk especially if you have excess facial hair already but the risk is low.

Uneven eyebrows

It is often unpredictable how your muscles will react especially in a new patient or new area in an existing patient. Your doctor did not do anything wrong; you just need a slight adjustment. Botox with bruised eye
