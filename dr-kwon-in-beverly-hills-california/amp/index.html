I have redness 5 weeks after an Asian rhinoplasty— could I have an infection?
Redness after Implant Rhinoplasty
Sep 16, 2020
It is not clear whether you have an infection. If the redness continues to get worse or the bump you have opens up and starts draining fluid, you are likely to have an implant infection.

The best course would be to see your surgeon and determine if antibiotics would be appropriate in your case. If the redness goes away after your antibiotics course and stays away, you are probably good. If, however, it gets better and returns or even worsens, you may have to consider implant removal.
