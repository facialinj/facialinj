If I have no thigh gap, will J Plasma help create one for me?
J Plasma for Thigh Gap
Jul 18, 2020
J plasma can help refine the thigh gap created by liposuction. Think of it this way, liposuction removes the fat and adipose tissue of the upper and inner thighs. Now just the loose skin is left hanging. J plasma will help improve the inner thigh contour and really tighten that skin up.

When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
How long does swelling last after breast augmentation?
Apr 13, 2020
This is a very common question. Most of the swelling subsides by 2-3 months, however it can take up to 6 months or longer for the final results to become refined. It is common for the breasts to look high in the early pre operative period due to a combination of swelling and tightness of the skin envelope. This resolves over the first month.
