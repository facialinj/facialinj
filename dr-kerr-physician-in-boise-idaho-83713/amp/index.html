Kybella Swelling
Apr 29, 2019
In our experience, patients tend to stay swollen/tender a little longer after their 2nd & 3rd treatments. Unfortunately, there’s no way to speed this up and every single patient is different. Two days is too soon to tell results from lip filler injections. In fact, usually swelling is at it's highest 2-4 days after and can cause a very uneven, lumpy/bumpy look.
