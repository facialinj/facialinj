Best solution for double bubble, rippling and animation deformity?
Revision
Nov 9, 2017
It appears that your muscle has been divided high on the sternum causing the muscle to window shade.  Which likely means that in essence the implants are “on top the muscle” in the area that is rippling.  I would change them out to allergan highly cohesive implants to minimize the rippling, change them to the subglandular plane to correct the animation deformity.  To address the implant displacement, the pocket needs to be adjusted.
