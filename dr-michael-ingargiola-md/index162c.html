Is a brachioplasty really effective?
Brachioplasty
Aug 27, 2020
Great question! Brachioplasty is a very effective surgery in removing excess/hanging skin and fat from the arm. How big the scar is really depends on how much and where the excess tissue is. We do our best to hide the scar on the inner arm and by the armpit which is not usually seen when you have your arms by your side. That being said, these scars can be large but with proper scar management and good surgical technique they can be minimized. I hope this helps!

Upper eyelids
Aug 24, 2020
