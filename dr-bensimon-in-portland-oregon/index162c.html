How Many Units to Botox Crow's Feet?
Crows Feet - or Volume Loss?
Oct 17, 2016
The FDA has approved 24 units of Botox (total) for crow's feet correction. However, keep in mind that as we age sometimes we need to readjust dosage or address loss of volume issues. Temporal hollows can be an easy treatment in conjunction with Botox to erase crows feet. Without seeing a photo of you it's hard to address if the issue could be loss of elasticity and volume around the eyes. Please consult with your Physician to guarantee your best treatment option.

Is there any recourse for Botox injections that do not work?
Guaranteed Botox Results
Oct 17, 2016
Botox can take a full 2 weeks to settle in and metabolize into the muscle. I recommend going back to the provider for a 2-week follow up to assure the correct dosage was injected. It's hard to assess without seeing "before" and "after" photos because each patient requires a different dosage for best results. At our surgical and aesthetic practice we guarantee our results with a 2-week follow up to ensure proper dosage.
