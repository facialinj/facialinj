I developed a rash on my back after using Rogaine. Should I quit using Rogaine entirely?
Rash after Rogaine
May 30, 2020
Rogaine is FDA approved to improve hair thinning. However the side effects of Rogaine can be increased hair growth in undesired places as well as irritation on the scalp and skin with topical application. It’s important to ensure that Rogaine solution is not dripping along your back which could be contributing to the irritation. You can discontinue the use of Rogaine to see if the rash resolves. if it doesn’t, get evaluated  by a board certified dermatologist.

Do post acne red marks get darker as they fade?
Acne scars
Jan 11, 2019

Can I have a tattoo and get my nose pierced a month before Accutane?

Jan 11, 2019
There should not be a problem if you do the tattoo and piercing a month before starting Isotretinoin as long as you are completely healed.  You should not consider any treatment during or for several months post taking Isotretinoin.Might be best to ask your Board Certified Dermatologist.
