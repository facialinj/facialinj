What are the benefits of Restylane Kysse in comparison to Juvederm Ultra Plus XC?
Restylane Kysse versus Juvederm Ultra
Jun 2, 2020
Both are good lip fillers as long as the treatment is performed by an experienced injector. I work with the Restylane family of products in my clinic and so I use a lot of Restylane Kysse. It gives a very natural looking result. You will also note improvement in lip texture and color after Kysse treatment. The results last up to a year.

Clear + Brilliant versus Halo
Jun 1, 2020
The Halo laser is a more aggressive treatment then Clear + Brilliant. I offer Clear + Brilliant in my office and like it as a gentle minimal downtime laser treatment that helps maintain youthful looking skin.
