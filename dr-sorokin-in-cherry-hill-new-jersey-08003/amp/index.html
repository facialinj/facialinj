8 months post breast augmentation- are my breasts malpositioned?
Consider a lift
Dec 2, 2018
The appearance of your breasts is a predictable result given the starting position and shape of your natural tissue.  The only way to move it up and towards the breast bone as you describe would be by doing a lift to reshape the natural tissue.  The downside of the lift is of course the incisions that are involved but otherwise the implant is just giving you a bigger version of where you started.  I hope this helps.  Best of luck!

Nov 16, 2018
Different surgeons have different protocols to return to exercise and it isn't just based on their "opinion" but rather the specific techniques that they do as well as the recoveries of their own patients that they have watched over the years.  I think 4-6 weeks after breast augmentation is a bit on the long side myself but that is not a universal recommendation and I would best advise speaking to your surgeon.  Best of luck!

Should I expect more pain in my revision surgery?

Nov 16, 2018
Your recovery will depend on what exactly the surgeon needs to do.  Some revisions are nearly painless while others can be quite dramatic depending on how scarred the contracture is and what the surgeon needs to do.  Best advice is to speak to your doc who knows his/her plan.  Best of luck!
