Pregnancy after getting fillers a while back, what are the risks?
Fillers and botox in pregnancy
Sep 14, 2020
I is never a good idea to get an elective cosmetic procedure while pregnant or nursing. Most physicians will not treat a women who is nursing or pregnant with botox or fillers. I am not aware of any studies that look at the risk associated with a woman has had these procedures and than becomes pregnant. In my practice I have had many patients who had botox and fillers and then went on to have uncomplicated pregnancies. You should discuss this with your physician if you have any concerns.
