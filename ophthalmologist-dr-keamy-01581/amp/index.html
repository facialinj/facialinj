Fillers for lines around mouth and Smile Lines

What treatment is recommended to look ten years younger?
Looking 10 years younger
Jun 23, 2019
I am a huge fan of IPL or Limelight to get rid of brown spots. I also like using it with Laser Genesis to stimulate collagen. Most of my patients also use either ZO Skin Care or Defense to stimulate more collagen and maintain a good complexion.
Is it okay that cheek is tender to touch 4 weeks after Voluma?
Tenderness After Voluma
Jun 23, 2019
I find that patients can have some tenderness after Voluma for several week. Since the injection is very deep, the needle can sometimes bruise the periosteum or bone. These injuries take awhile to feel better. Usually motrin will help with the pain.
There is a rare risk that you could have an infection. Given that possibility, you should see your provider for an evaluation.
I got this after voluma injection. What is it? It will resolve with time?
Voluma Lumps
Jun 23, 2019
When I inject Voluma, I inject little amounts in balls in the areas I want to lift. Initially there is swelling in the area and some lumpiness. Usually over the first 3 weeks, the swelling and lumps smooth out. By a month, the cheek should feel smooth. If you still have a lump in 3 weeks, your provider can massage it to integrate into the skin or dissolve it with hylenex.
Is there a difference between how deep voluma vs. Radiesse is injected in the temples?
Depth of Voluma and Radiesse
Jun 23, 2019
Both Radiesse and Voluma are good fillers for the cheeks ( zygoma) and the temples. Both offer a lift to the tissue. When injecting in these two areas, both Radiesse and Voluma are injected very deep just above the periosteum. Some other hyaluronic acid fillers can be injected more superficially; however, radiesse must alway be deep. Radiesse is not indicated for superficial lines. It is white and would be visible if injected more superficially.
Can different types of filler be used in the same area (e.g. Radiesse + Voluma)?
Voluma in the Temples
Jun 23, 2019
Voluma in the temples is smooth an beautiful. It is my favorite for that area and in the cheeks. Filling the temples can make you look a decade younger. There is no reason you cannot have your temples injected with Voluma. For some people, Radiesse does not last that long. Voluma should last 1-2 years in the temples.
How to add Voluma to an area while avoiding new scars from the needle?
Cannula injections for Voluma
Jun 23, 2019
I am sorry to hear that you scar from needles on your face. At least one needle mark must be made to inject voluma either through strands with a needle or with a blunt cannula under the skin. If the needle or cannula cannot reach all areas that need to be injection, additional needles may be needed to inject the Voluma in the targeted area.
Is Bellafill a safe under eye filler?
Bellafill Not for Under the Eyes
Jun 23, 2019
Injections under the eyes for so called "bags" should only be done by very experienced injectors. Bellafill cannot be reversed and is not indicated for under the eyes. Personally I like Restylane under the eyes either by injection with a needle or cannula.
Is Bellafill right for me?
Bellafill for Acne scars
Jun 23, 2019
I find Bellafill to work very well in acne scars. It also lasts longer than any other filler. My patients comment on how smooth their skin is after treatment.
Bellafill for upper lip lines or skin resurfacing?
Bellafill for above the Lip Lines
Jun 23, 2019
I find Bellafill is ideal for the lines above the upper lip. Typically I use a cannula to inject it in this area. I find it stimulates collagen and smooths out the entire area. It cannot be used inside the lip just around the lips.
Can I have Bellafill injected after Juvederm for acne scarring?
Bellafill for Scars
Jun 23, 2019
After a year the Juvederm should be almost gone so I do not see any contraindication to using Bellafill this time.
Does Bellafill for acne scars only last 1 year?
Bellafill for Scars
Jun 23, 2019
Bellafill helps smooth out scars by stimulating collagen. It can be effective up to 5 years. However, each patient has a different response to Bellafill. Currently it has the longest duration of any filler.

Dysport on Chin
Mar 2, 2019
It takes at least 7-14 days for dysport to have its full effect. As the muscle freezes, I have found it may freeze unevenly at first but usually smooths out. If you are not happy with results in 2 weeks, I would contact your provider.
Why is Restylane Lyft is not recommended for the under eye area ?
Restylane Under the Eyes
Feb 28, 2019
Restylane Lyft for most people is too thick for the thin skin under the eyes. I personally prefer Restylane, If you are happy with the restylane lyft, for nowI would do nothing. If for some reason you do not like it, it can be dissolved with hylenex.

botox in forehead
