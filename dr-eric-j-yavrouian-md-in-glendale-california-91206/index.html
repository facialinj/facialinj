Will weight loss make my nose look thinner, or do I need to consider rhinoplasty?
weight and the appearance of the nose
Oct 3, 2018
Weight gain or loss will not change the shape or size of the nose. However, your face may change with weight loss, and can affect the way the nose appears in proportion to the rest of your face. Weight loss may actually make your nose appear larger, and weight gain may make it appear smaller.
What Foods Should One Eat After Rhinoplasty?
Diet after rhinoplasty
Oct 3, 2018
After your rhinoplasty, its best to avoid hot and spicy foods and big bits that lead to excess stretch of your upper lip. This is most important the first week or two after surgery. In terms of straining with bowel movements, consider an over the counter stool softener especially if you are also on narcotics for pain management.
Sleeping Position After Rhinoplasty?
Sleeping position after Rhinoplasty
Oct 3, 2018
After rhinoplasty, it is best to sleep with your head elevated the first 10 days after surgery. This helps reduce swelling and potential injury. After then, if you can continue to sleep in that position, it is best but not absolutely necessary. As far as other positions, unless you are putting quite a bit of pressure on the nose, it should not effect your result.
Is ear cartilage better than rib cartilage for a rhinoplasty if someone has thin skin?
Ear vs Rib Cartilage
Oct 3, 2018
When septal cartilage is unavailable, rib or ear cartilage can be used for grafts. The decision as to which depends on the amount of cartilage needed and the strength/type of grafts needed for the rhinoplasty. Rib cartilage is useful when in need a larger amount of grafts and those with greater strength. The thickness of the skin does not affect the source of the cartilage used; it alters the types of grafts placed. Thank you for the question. Good luck with your surgery!

Tip refinement

With a rhinoplasty, you can definitely refine your tip and nostrils. It is best to seek a consultation with a board certified surgeon who is an expert in rhinoplasty but who also does computer imaging during the consultation as this can help with surgical planning and common goal for the outcome.
When Did Swelling Go Down After Your Rhinoplasty Surgery?
Swelling 2 weeks after rhinoplasty
Oct 3, 2018
It is very normal, even expected for the nose to swell more after removing the cast as the cast is what helps keep the swelling out. It takes a full year to recover from rhinoplasty. About 90% of the swelling should resolve in the first three months, with the remaining 10% over the next 9 months. The swelling takes the longest to resolve in the nasal tip. You should see major improvement with time. 
Swollen and Big Nose Tip After 3 Months of Rhinoplasty?
Tip swelling after Rhinoplasty
Oct 3, 2018
It can take at least a year for a nose to heal after a primary rhinoplasty and sometimes longer especially if you are thick skinned. At three months out, you have quite a bit of swelling in your nasal tip and it should improve with time. However, it is best to follow up with your surgeon so that he or she can address your concerns.

Can touching your nose after rhinoplasty affect your results?
Touching nose after rhinoplasty
Nov 7, 2017
After a rhinoplasty, you want to be very careful with your nose. It is best to be gentle when cleaning your nose. You should avoid squeezing it or moving it while it is healing as the sutures and grafts used during a rhinoplasty are delicate. You should contact your surgeon for the changes you are noticing and have him or her evaluate you in person. 
Rhinoplasty Swelling
Swelling after rhinoplasty
Oct 10, 2017
Swelling is common and the majority resolves within the first three months. The first month after a rhinoplasty, the nose is the most swollen. After about three months, 90% of the swelling usually resolves with the nasal tip as the last place for the swelling to improve. It takes a full year for the swelling to fully resolve and to have your final result.
