Can I get 3500 cc from fat grafting? Have thick core and space to fill in breasts
Fat transfer to breasts
Sep 16, 2020
You describe very large breasts on a large frame. Fat transfer would not likely be useful for shaping large, heavy breasts, particularly since you also describe them as "very saggy". Please have a consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Nobody should be putting 3500 cc of fat into your breasts; it's just a bad idea and invites lots of potential problems (oil cysts, fat necrosis).
I am researching what needs to be done to fix my muscle flex deformity
Animation of chest after augmentation
Sep 16, 2020
Thank you for your questions and helpful photos. You have an animation deformity caused by muscle contraction sqeezing your implants. The best solution for you at this point will likely be a pocket change to move the implants above the muscle. You are thin, so you'd also likely benefit from some fat transfer around the implants, so they edges aren't so visible above the muscle. You may also need a breast lift to create an ideal shape, but that's something to discuss with your surgeon.

Yes, you can have liposuction even if you have loose skin.  BUT you will not be flat, tight, and smooth at the end.  Your loose skin will still be there.  If you want flat, tight, and smooth, you'll need to also address the loose skin.
Is a nipple graft different than adjusting the nipple upwards during a breast reduction? Is this alwa
Nipple graft versus nipple repositioning
Mar 26, 2020
A nipple graft means removing the whole areola and nipple, then sewing it back into a new area of the breast.  This is usually done when blood supply is a concern.Nipple repositioning is always done as part of a breast lift or breast reduction, and it does not involve removing the areola and nipple, just repositioning it.

Weight Loss

For sustained weight loss, I would recommend seeing a nutritionist who can help you plan your meals.  Keeping a food log can show you where calories are sneaking in that add up to more than you think--try something like myfitnesspal .  Starting and sustaining an exercise plan is also important.  These are lifestyle changes, not "diets or pills".  Good luck!

Can you change your lip shape into another person's own?
Changing lip shape
Mar 26, 2020
Aside from fillers, there are surgical procedures that can change the lip shape.  A lip lift can increase the pink height of the upper lip.  A corner lip lift can change the angle of the corners of the mouth.  These leave scars, of course, but they should ideally be thin and pale.  You'll need a consultation to determine what's reasonable and realistic.
