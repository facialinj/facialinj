Will two PDO threads make a noticeable difference in my lower face?
PDO threads along will not give the best results for you
Feb 24, 2020
Your mid face must be supported as well for your lower face to improve. Remember our aging process involves loss of volume and then falls downwards. For the best balance results you will need volume replace in the mid face and extend of chin first, then threads will lift and support the lower face.
How to enhance my neck area/jawline at 27?
Y lift to create definition
Feb 24, 2020
I would not use threads on you since it would not give you the defined jewline. My first recommendation would be using the Y lift technique to enhance the chin forward and creating the jew/mandibular angle. Next is lipolysis with Coolsculpting, kybella, or smartlipo depending on my examination of the area.
After liposuction, I have dimpling/lumpiness and shading/discoloration on my front thighs. Will J-Plasma
Not likely
Nov 12, 2019
The liposuction performed 2 years ago was too aggressive and too superficial. The shading and discoloration most likely due to damage of the tissue under the skin. This is very difficult to fix. I would try PRP injections every 4-6 weeks times 3. May need to perform lipo shifting after to smooth out. I don't think Renuvion will improve your problem.
How long will I have swelling and scars after Smart Lipo of lower abdomen and flanks?
2-4 weeks
Nov 3, 2019
My experience over the last 15 years performing smartlipo would suggest you should heal well by 2-4 weeks. 28 year old and healthy, good skin and minimal fat, no history of pregnancy are all positive signs of quick recovery. Usually min to no bruising. It is important to wear the compression garment for the first 2 weeks and no exercise, hot tub, swimming.
Will I get an hourglass shape from Smart Lipo? Will my results be noticeable? Do you think I’m a good can
Yes you are
Nov 3, 2019
You waist and flanks should have good results from smart lipo. It will create a hourglass shape and enhance your hip and butt curves. Your abdomen also should have good results, but form the pictures it is hard to differentiate how much internal fat you have. I have been performing smart lipo for 15 years and 90% of cases would be both abdomen and flanks together at the same visit. It produces the best over all result for the mid section.
Xeomin vs. Botox - Which is Better?
Botox vs Xeomin
Oct 29, 2019
I have been injecting for the past 15 years. I use both Botox and Xeomin and find there are some clinical differences. Botox has more of the stiff tight frozen feel and can cause some people to have headaches. It is more effective in arm pit excessive sweating and muscle reduction. Xeomin has a softer feel that most first time patients is comfortable with. The duration of both is about the same, since I have used it on same people.
Will getting a retouch make my muscles stay smaller for a longer time?
Masseter reduction
Oct 29, 2019
It would be helpful to see the before and after photos. your facial features are asymmetrical which is very common, and just using xeomin will not make both sides look the same.
Will Smart Lipo 360 Work? 42F, 171 lbs, BMI 30
J plasma or Renuvion will work
Oct 29, 2019
TT would give you great results but with large scar, down time and major risk. J plasma/Renuvion plus liposuction will give you about 70 to 80 % of a TT. the muscle weakness can be improved with CoolTone after the lipo. Only you can make the best choice for yourself. The bra and waist area will have significant improvement as well.
Am I a good candidate for smart lipo on the abdomen? I am 35 and have 2 children.
Abdomen after 2 pregnancy
Oct 29, 2019
You have a common problem after pregnancy. You have very little subQ fat, skin is stretched out and abdomen muscle is weak. Your best option other then tummy tuck is Liposuction with smart lipo or Renuvion first. Cooltone or Emsculpt to strengthen the muscle. Superficial treatment for the stretch marks with microneedle radio frequency such as Vivace or secret.

Smart Lipo
Oct 29, 2019
I would always try to address what bothers you the most. It sounds like your Abdomen is your main concern. How much does Smart Lipo to the arms cost and does it leave scarring?
Samrt Lipo Arms

Liposuction Vs. SmartLipo - Which is Better?
Smart Lipo
Oct 29, 2019
Smart Lipo includes Liposuction. The best results can be achieved when artistic liposuction is performed and the Smart Lipo or Renuvion added to help tighten the skin. I prefer to perform these procedures under local tumescent anesthesia to reduce major complications.
Will this procedure really help me scale down my thighs?
Smart Lipo of thighs
Oct 29, 2019
Smart Lipo is used to remove subcutaneous fat and help shrink the skin. If you have large thighs due to large muscles then it will not reduce the size. It is very important to contour and shape the buttuck and thigh together so the desired curves are achieved. after consultation you should have a very good understanding of what the final results will be. If your not sure then keep looking for other options.
How Much Does Smart Lipo Cost?
Smart Lipo cost
Oct 29, 2019
I perform Smart Lipo under local Anastasia only for the past 15 years. Each body area is around $3500. Most people would have up to 3 areas done at the same time which adds up to average of $7,000 to $9,000. This includes all expense such as compression garment and follow up visits.
Smart Lipo incisions areas for the lower back and flanks?
incision sites for you
Oct 29, 2019
I have been performing Smart Lipo for the last 15 years and have many smaller size patients like you. My most common incision site are on each mid low back area about 3 mm. On larger or more skin rolls then I would add additional incision site and use J-plasma/renuvion to achieve the best result.
