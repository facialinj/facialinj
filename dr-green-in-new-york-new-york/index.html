Co2 laser treatment
Aug 9, 2020
Co2 laser treatment is the most aggressive form of laser treatment for acne scars. The redness can stay for several months. It may not get rid of all depressed or atrophic scars, since it depends on the volume lost and the depth of your acne scars. Most patients opt for less aggressive lasers like Fraxel or eMatrix for acne scars, since they do not want the down time of traditional C02 lasers. Best, Dr. Green
