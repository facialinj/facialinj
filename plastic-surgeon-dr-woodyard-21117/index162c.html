How do you create symmetry with implants when the breasts are significantly different diameters?

Breast asymmetry can be corrected using different size implants if the asymmetry is significant. A small discrepancy from one side to the other is not unusual in nature. If 1 breast is larger than the other, some of that extra tissue can be removed or a smaller implant can be used on that side. Is breast implants after Pulmonary Embolism possible?
breast implants after Pulmonary Embolism possible?
Oct 17, 2019
Yes, you can undergo breast augmentation after a pulmonary embolism if the right precautions are taken. The 1st person you should check with is your hematologist to see what their recommendations are regarding this relatively short operation. Luckily you don't have an underlying genetic predisposition so your risks should be similar to the general population. Having had an embolism in the past, does increase your risk and that just needs to be taken into consideration.
