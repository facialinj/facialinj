Treatment of ice pick acne scars cross TCA fractional RF
May 28, 2020
Ice pick acne scars are best treated with cross TCA for deeper ones, sometimes excision, fractional radiofrequency, microneedling, profractional, or fraxel laser.  Combination treatments work best.  PRP can be added for more benefit.
