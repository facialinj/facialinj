FaceTite v Accutite? Would it give the same results?

Jul 28, 2020
The difference between AccuTite and FaceTite are the sizes of the handpieces. Accutite is much smaller and it is generally used to treat small areas like the nasolabial fold, knees, lower and upper eyelids. AccuTite can also treat the jowls and neck. The FaceTite hand piece is bigger and treats the neck and jowls. It cannot treats the smaller areas on the face because of it's size. The Radiofrequency used is the same.
