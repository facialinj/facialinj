Are there side effects for having Botox for a prolonged period of time and then stopping?
What Happens when you stop Botox Injections?
Feb 16, 2019
You will NOT have worse facial wrinkles or droopy skin from doing Botox injections and then stopping. Botox buys you "WRINKLE FREE" time. So, your wrinkles and skin will be better with botox than they would be if you never did it. However, since Botox makes wrinkles better, and keeps your skin lifted, stopping it means wrinkles will start to appear, and drooping will start to happen. The wrinkles and drooping aren't because you used Botox, they are because you have stopped using it.
