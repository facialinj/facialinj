How can bad filler be fixed? It has distorted my face but it’s in a fatty area so I’m nervous to dissol
Can bad filler be dissolved?
Aug 31, 2020
If the filler was a hyaluronic acid (HA) filler, then it can be dissolved by injecting hyaluronidase into the area. Or you could give it some time and let the filler dissolve on its own over time. Examples of HA fillers are Juvederm and Restylane. There are other fillers that cannot be dissolved. You should try to find out what type of filler you had injected. This would allow your doctor to give a more accurate recommendation for you.
Regards,
Dr. Ort

Aug 31, 2020
Yes I believe that subcision would help your acne scars. I usually prefer to do a blunt subcision using a cannula rather than a sharp needle. This helps to reduce downtime and any side effects.
