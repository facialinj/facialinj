Apr 15, 2020
It takes approximately 6 weeks for the 3rd molar tooth sockets to fill in with gum tissue. They heal from the bottom up so it is normal to have a “divot” in the gum tissue for a month or two after surgery. You can eat anything you like that is comfortable. I recommend avoiding hard, crunchy foods with lots of bits and crumbs, like chips, nuts, cereal until the sockets are closed and avoid straws for the 1st week after surgery.
