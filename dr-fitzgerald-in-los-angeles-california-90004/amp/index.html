Is this brown spot an age spot?
The Sun and Brown Spots – Not a Good Combination
Sep 7, 2020
Any pigmented spots or areas will get darker with sun exposure. We often refer to age spots and sun spots individually but they are generally one and the same – a response by the skin to protect itself.

Age spots come over time with repeated sun exposure. Sun spots do too, but you can get dark spots for all kinds of reasons. The sun will simply make them worse.

The best habit you can get into is using sunscreen. Sunbathing might feel good, but it’s not good for your skin.
