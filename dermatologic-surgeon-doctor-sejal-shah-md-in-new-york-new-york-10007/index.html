Will laser removal leave a scar?
Laser tattoo removal and scarring
Dec 30, 2016
Thank you for your photos and question. Generally the risk of scarring is very low. However, some people do develop fibrosis, or thickening of the skin which feels like a scar, after tattoo removal. The best way to reduce this risk is to see a board certified expert who uses a picosecond laser, such as the Enlighten. You will require less treatments and therefore have less risk of scarring.

Good Luck!
