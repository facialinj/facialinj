Under Eye Wrinkles

Why don’t doctors aspirate with dermal filler?
Aspiration
Dec 6, 2018
Thanks for your question. Once the needle or cannula has dermal filler in it aspiration is completely unreliable as a "safety measure". Some still demonstrate it as a "show" but the fact is it is quite useless. There are other technique ex we use to reduce risk, such as avoiding known danger zones, constantly moving the top while injecting, being at the proper depth on a region. Etc.. that keep the incidence of vascular occlusion very rare.
Best,
Lisa Vuich. MD

Can AquaGold microinfusion improve the looks of rolling and boxed acne scars on Asian skin?
Aquagold for Acne scars
Nov 26, 2018
Thanks for your question. The needle penetration of the Aquagold device is very superficial. It helps penetration of facial products (serums for example) but would not be expected to remodel acne scarring no matter how many you did.
Traditional microneedling devices, along with platelet rich plasma, would be a more effective choice with minimal downtime.
Best,
Lisa Vuich, MD
How fast after fillers can blindness occur?
Blindness after filler
Nov 19, 2018
Thanks for your question. Blindness as a filler complication is very rare and most often associated with filler placed in between the brows or very close to the nose, the "danger zones". If it were to occur, it would be immediate onset, not hours and days later.
Best,
Lisa Vuich, MD
I did Ultherapy but the doctor didn't use 4.5mm head for my treatment. Is this usual？
Ultherapy protocol
Nov 19, 2018
Thanks for your question. It is correct that the Ultherapy treatment heads used are dictated by the thickness of the tissue. The doctor can see the target layer with the ultrasound and choose the right tip to reach it. Going "too deep" delivers energy to the bone, which does nothing for a lift as you can imagine. You would not be expected to see anything at this point. Wait 3 months to redo your photos.
