How could upper blepharoplasty surgery impact tear production?

It is unlikely that surgery has caused any dry eye. Blepharoplasty would not affect tear production. You might have a closure problem or poor blink from overaggressive surgery. Pre existing dry eye or poor baseline tear production from previous LASIK can make this worse. You should see a fellowship trained Oculoplastic surgeon who is ASOPRS affiliated.
