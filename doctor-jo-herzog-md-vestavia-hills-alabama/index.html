Pregnancy after getting fillers a while back, what are the risks?
Fillers/Botox before pregnancy
Sep 14, 2020
Most physician do not like to do elective cosmetic procedures on pregnant patients. Miscarriage and birth defects are non uncommon and if someone has something done while pregnant and then has a negative outcome they will forever wonder whether there was a relationship. I do not know of any studies showing a danger of this or of any reports of negative outcome. I do not think that any of these treatments should be a problem prior to pregnancy .
