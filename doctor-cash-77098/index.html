I lost ~ten pounds and my breasts decreased around 3 inches. Is this normal?
Weight loss and breast size
Sep 29, 2020
We are each unique in how our bodies respond when we gain or lose weight. Some lose it in their face, others in the abdomen, others in their breasts, etc. There is no way to predict if you will gain the breast volume back if you gain the weight you lost.

Is it okay to be nervous about breast augmentation surgery?
Nervous
Sep 29, 2020
It is completely normal to be somewhat nervous about the consultation and the surgery. During the consultation you will have an opportunity to learn more about the procedure as well as discuss your concerns and get answers to your questions. Be sure to select a board certified plastic surgeon for your procedure.

Breast options

I have baggy eyes and dark circles what is the best treatment for this?
Under eye concerns
Sep 29, 2020
The look of under eye bags can be improved with fillers. They work by using volume to disguise under eye contour irregularities. Sometimes dark circles are caused by shadowing from lack of volume under the eye. This is also improved with fillers. The treatment is done in the office and the results last up to a year.
“Implant illness” groups made me inundated w fear. Set to explant. (Smthsaline 13y). Why such dif opin
Implant illness
Sep 29, 2020
If you are having your implants removed under local anesthesia the capsules will be left behind. General anesthesia is recommended in order to remove both the implants and capsules. Typically capsules should be removed if there are issues with the implants including rupture, capsular contracture, etc. If there are no issues then it is optional to have the implants removed or leave them behind.

What is the most effective treatment for my under-eye crepey skin?
Crepey skin
Sep 29, 2020
Crepey skin under the eye can be addressed with lasers. They work well to improve texture and fine lines. You can consider fillers under the eye to add volume and improve contour irregularities. Neuromodulators such as Botox can help soften dynamic lines at the outer part of the eye ("Crow's feet') as well as between the brows and on the forehead.
What can help the lines for my crows feet and the sagging under eye?
Considering procedures
Sep 29, 2020
You may be a better candidate for lower blepharoplasty. This surgery will remove excess skin and lost volume can be replaced using your own fat to give a smoother contour under the eye. Neuromodulators such as Botox and Xeomin work well to soften dynamic lines at the outer eye. See a board certified plastic surgeon near you to learn more.

Laser resurfacing will help improve the crepey skin that concerns you. These lasers are specially designed and safe to treat on delicate skin in trained hands. We also recommend using medical grade skin care products and wearing sunscreen every day.
1 Week Post Op How Will Crescent Lift Look with Removal of Implants?
Breast implant removal
Sep 29, 2020
I'm sorry you are unhappy with your breast surgery. I encourage you to discuss your concerns with your surgeon before making a final decision. If you remove the implants you will have a look similar to that prior to implants. The crescent lift is a conservative approach and would not provide a significant lifted look. The incision should still heal normally even with the implants removed.
What happens to my body if silicone implants in my body are damaged/broken?
Ruptured implants
Sep 29, 2020
If there is a rupture to the gel type silicone implants, the cohesive silicone gel is designed to not leak out of the shell. If it does, often the silicone remains within the confines of the scar capsule. In the case of ruptured implants, it is recommended that the implant and capsule are removed as one unit, called en bloc, as soon as it's reasonably possible.
