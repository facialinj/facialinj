Will temple filler and neurotoxin be able to balance my eye and brow asymmetry?
Fillers
Apr 15, 2019
There are some great Fillers on the market. Filler like Voluma or Bellafill for the temple area, work great. Botox can help with crows feet and asymmetry. I love meeting my clients and taking my time with recommending the right filler for them. We can always add more, and I like my clients to take their time, and feel happy with the results. I always recommend a natural look, so they look like a better version of themselves.
How can I get firmer skin and a more feminine look?
Firmer Skin and A More Feminine Look with Fillers
Apr 15, 2019
To get skin firmer I would recommend a good skin program. After a detailed evaluation, we like combining treatments like peel and microneedling / vampire facials. The treatment plan you select, depends on how much down time you have. Results from Smart Skin CO2 is amazing, and a one time good deep treatment for skin resurfacing. I love achieve long lasting results and hightly recommend a detailed treatment plan.
Nasolabial folds, jowling, and midface volume loss. What is the best option for all these?
Mid-Face Volume Loss
Apr 15, 2019
You need a detail evaluation of the amount of volume loss. I prefer a combination treatment of filler along with PDO Threads. Mid face volume loss accentuates nasolabial folds, and they look deeper. It’s the lift we want, that's why I recommend fillers and thread lift that result in a much more natural look.
Can a HydraFacial be done to prepare for a chemical peel on the same day? Are there gentler ways
HydraFacial and Chemical Peels Together
Mar 26, 2019
Yes extractions can be done at same time, of course all skin types react differently. As per procedure after a Chemical Peel it is important to stay out of the sun for at least 24 hours and wear sunblock.
We always offer a good Microderm or HydroFacial before peel. It exfoliates and hydrates the skin and when you apply peel it goes deeper and responds well. Again consult in person since everyone’s skin type and age can change the treatment plan.

HELP! Will my acne scars ever go away?
Acne Scars

Why does my skin feel so tight when I take my compression suit off?
Compression
Nov 11, 2018
The skin is still healing and feels firm because of swelling and underlying bruising. There are no negative effects of Smart Lipo on skin, it only improves collagen, and can cause tightening in the areas and improve stretch marks. I encourage my patients to get lymphatic massages afterwards, or add something like Venus freeze to quicken the healing process and these modalities complement the procedure well .

SmartLipo and Belly Fat

Smart Lipo works well for small stubborn areas like the abdomen (belly) usually responds well unless someone has gained weight, Smart Lipo is not a substitute for diet and excercise. Jowls Are Starting to Appear, What Are The Best Options?
Problem Jowl Area
Oct 30, 2018
We start to see prominent jowls when we age. The volume loss in cheeks, loose skin and lack of collagen production are typical signs of aging. I would start a treatment plan with volume correction, using filler and add threads for lower face lift.
How Long Do Injectable Fillers REALLY Last?
Do Fillers Last
Oct 30, 2018
The life-span of a filler depends on the person and the product, for example Juverderm typically lasts about 8 months and Voluma last upto 2 years. Bellafil is a longer lasting filler, but I always recommend that all clients require maintenance depending on how much correction they have had.
Can Fillers Take Care of Vertical Lips Lines?
Vertical Lip Lines
Oct 30, 2018
Fillers can correct verticals lines; I recommend using a filler like Vobella or Juverderm in that area. CO2 Laser can reduce these lines also.
What are these lines on my cheeks and how do I get rid of or lessen them?
Cheek Area
Oct 30, 2018
Simply put, the lines on cheek are from volume loss, sun damage and crows feet, all part of the aging process. I would start with Botox, depending on what volume loss I see. I would then correct volume loss.
